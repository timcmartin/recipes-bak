# Thai Shrimp Skewers

## Ingredients
* Free form:
  * Lime Juice
  * Sriracha
  * Ketchup
  * Sweet Thai Chili Sauce
  * 1/4 cup sesame oil
  * 1/4 cup canola oil
  * 1/4 cup soya sauce

## Directions
1. Skewer shrimp
2. 10 minutes before ready to grill, pour 1/2 to 2/3 of the marinade over the shrimp.
3. Grill on medium high heat, brushing the remaining marinade on while grilling.
