# Pancakes

## Ingredients
* 10 ounces flour
* 3 tbsp sugar
* 4 tsp baking powder
* 1/2 tsp baking soda
* 1 tsp salt
* Whisk to combine
* 1/4 cup vegetable oil
* 2 eggs
* Whisk
* 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
* 1.5 cup milk added slowly as you whisk

## Directions
1. Combine wet and dry ingredients and stir gently to combine, leaving lumps is okay.
